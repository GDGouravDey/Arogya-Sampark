Departments

General Surgery Department

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Subhankar Ray

General surgery involves a range of surgical procedures focused on the abdomen and its organs, including the digestive tract. Surgeons in this department address various medical conditions and perform surgeries for treatment.

Cardiology Department

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Manoj Baidya

Cardiology deals with the study and treatment of heart-related conditions. Cardiologists diagnose and manage heart diseases, cardiac emergencies, heart failure, arrhythmias, and other cardiovascular disorders.

Department of Pediatrics

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Harsh Kumar

Pediatrics Department is the medical specialty focused on the health and well-being of infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatricians provide preventive care, manage illnesses, and address the unique health needs of young patients.

Neurology Department

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Arnab Santra

The Neurology Department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Neurologists manage conditions like epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological disorders.

Oncology Department

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Mitali Das

Oncology is the branch of medicine that deals with the study, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer. Oncologists specialize in various types of cancer, including their prevention, diagnosis, treatment planning, and ongoing care.

Department of Orthopaedics

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Sambhav Agarwal

Orthopaedics is mainly concerned with the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. Orthopedic surgeons diagnose and treat conditions related to the musculoskeletal system, such as fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries.

Urology Department

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Susobhan Dey

Urology involves the study and treatment of the urinary tract in both males and females. Urologists manage conditions affecting the kidneys, bladder, prostate, and reproductive organs, as well as urinary tract infections and kidney stones.

Department of Psychiatry

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Sraboni Dutta

The Psychiatry Department focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental health disorders. Psychiatrists assess patients, provide counseling, prescribe medications, and design treatment plans to help individuals with various mental health conditions.

Ophthalmology Department

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Debjit Sanyal

Ophthalmology deals with study and treatment of eye diseases. Ophthalmologists diagnose and manage conditions like cataracts, glaucoma, retinal issues, and other vision problems.

Emergency Department (ED)

Head of Department (HOD) - Dr. Nilratan Mondal

The Emergency Department (ED) is the first point of contact for individuals seeking immediate medical attention. It addresses urgent medical conditions, injuries, and trauma, providing immediate care and stabilization before further evaluation and treatment.
